# Duke Nukem Manhattan Project

I played Duke Nukem: Manhattan Project a lot when I was a kid.

In 2004, getting computer time itself felt lucky. I was confused at first and it took me a while to understand the babe bombs and the key system.

Even now, all levels are burned in my memory. I still remember the sounds and all the small gameplay details.

I recently learned more from the Duke Nukem wiki too, like [Wozma](https://dukenukem.fandom.com/wiki/Wozma) and the [X-3000](https://dukenukem.fandom.com/wiki/Weapons_in_Duke_Nukem:_Manhattan_Project#X-3000) secret weapon.

I also finally defeated Wozma and unlocked the X-3000.

I can still recall the full chapter run:

1. Rooftop Rebellion
2. Chinatown Chiller
3. Metro Mayhem
4. Unholy Underworld
5. Fearsome Factory
6. Tanker Trouble
7. Deviant Drilling
8. Orbital Oblivion

Each chapter had 3 parts, so it always felt like a proper journey, not a quick run.

Seeing it on the [Mac App Store](https://apps.apple.com/in/app/duke-nukem-manhattan-project/id896145747) in 2026 was a very nice surprise.

All secrets. All kills. All nukes. Hard mode done.

I still love this game.
